Welcome to Research Ease

ResearchEase is designed to simplify the process of collecting data, without diminishing reliability or validity. It is particularly useful with field research or focus groups for qualitative data collection. Rather than solely using recording devices to collect responses, followed by transcription before analysis can be accomplished, ResearchEase allows you to create your research protocol in it's program. While conducting a focus group session, your participants are able to enter their responses using their smart phone, tablet or laptop, which then become entries in your database in real time. It is a useful tool across a variety of fields. Sign up to see the possibilities.
