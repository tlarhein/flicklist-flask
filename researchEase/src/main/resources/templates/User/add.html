Research Ease

Designed to simplify the process of collecting data without diminishing reliability, Research Ease is particularly useful for qualitative methods. Rather than solely using recording devices to collect responses, followed by transcription before analysis can be accomplished, ResearchEase allows you to create your research protocol in it's program. While conducting a focus group session, your participants are able to enter their responses using their smart phone, tablet or laptop, which then become entries in your database in real time. It is a useful tool across a variety of fields. Identifying hot spots for crime analysis? Enter addresses while in the field. Staff training? Marketing analysis? Create a user account to begin.
